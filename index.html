📜 How to Play

  • 🍄 Collect mushrooms for points (10–50 pts each)
  • ⚡ Collect quickly for a time bonus multiplier!
  • 🐍 Avoid the snake — it tracks you!
  • 🪨 Avoid rocks & logs or lose points
  • 🏁 Reach the right edge to complete the level
  • 💾 Save your profile to track your best progress
  • 🔊 Click speaker icon to adjust Music & SFX volume
  • 🎵 Press M to mute/unmute all audio
